About Me

I'm a currently a student at The University of Chicago, slated to graduate in 2017. I also work as a game developer at DoSomething.org. I'm interested in how humans interact with technology, and explore this by approaching programming with an artistic eye. This interest has lead me to investigate how interactive media and gender interact; harnessing technology for positive social impact; and the digital humanities.

I'm livestreaming life @jazzmyth, and you can see my Github projects under jmithani.
