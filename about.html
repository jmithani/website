About Me

I'm a currently an undergraduate at The University of Chicago, slated to graduate in 2017. I work as a game developer at DoSomething.org, creating digital games for positive social impact. I'm interested in how humans interact with technology, and explore this by approaching programming with an artistic eye. This has lead me to investigate how interactive media and gender interact; how to harness technology for social change; and the application of digital humanities.

I'm livestreaming life @jazzmyth, and you can see my Github projects at @jmithani.
